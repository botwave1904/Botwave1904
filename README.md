# Botwave

**AI that answers your phone 24/7. Sends quotes in 30 seconds. Books jobs while you sleep.**

---

## The Problem

You're on a job. Phone rings. You can't answer — hands are wet, or you're under a sink. Call goes to voicemail.

**85% of those callers never call back.** They call the next plumber on Google.

**That's $86,000/year in lost revenue.** Per contractor.

---

## What Botwave Does

| Feature | What Happens |
|---------|--------------|
| **Answers Calls 24/7** | Customer texts or calls. Bot responds instantly — day or night. |
| **Sends Instant Quotes** | "Kitchen sink clogged" → $196–$468 quote in seconds. Uses YOUR rates. |
| **Photo-to-Bid** | Customer sends job site photo. Bot returns professional PDF estimate in 30 seconds. |
| **Books Appointments** | Self-service scheduling. No back-and-forth texts. |
| **Collects Payments** | Stripe integration. Deposits paid before you arrive. |
| **Manages Crew** | Clock in/out, payroll, materials — all via text. |

---

## How It Works

**Customer Experience:**
1. Customer has a problem (leak, electrical issue, AC broken)
2. They text or call your Botwave number
3. Bot answers instantly, diagnoses the issue
4. Bot sends quote using your actual pricing
5. Customer books appointment and pays deposit
6. You get notified. Job is on your calendar.

**Your Experience:**
1. We onboard you (60-90 min VIP session)
2. We organize your business files
3. We set up your pricing in the system
4. We give you a dedicated number
5. You keep working. Bot handles the phone.
